How to Use this Website:

Each item in the list has one of three colour codes.

Red indicates an important item, teal indicates a less important one, and grey indicates a finished item.

To add a new item, click the new item button and press add once you have typed in the entry.

Each item originally appears as red. To change the importance, tap once for teal, twice for grey, and thrice to remove the item.
